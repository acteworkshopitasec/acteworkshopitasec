To Be Defined

The "Cyber Threats' Evolution (ACTE)" workshop focuses on emerging cyber threats and innovative defense strategies, aligning with Italy's National Cybersecurity Strategy (2022-2026). It enables Ph.D. researchers in cybersecurity to present findings, collaborate, and share insights with experts, advancing solutions to anticipate and counter cyber threats.
